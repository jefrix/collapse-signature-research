# Stream Two Taxonomy: The Four Levers of Technocratic Agency

To map the "Technocratic Class" within the Collapse Signature framework, we categorize principals by their primary lever of civilizational influence.

### 1. Capital Allocators
**Lever**: The movement and concentration of liquidity.
**Function**: Pricing the future. They determine which systems are funded for resilience and which are left to simplify.
**Seed**: Larry Fink.

### 2. Frontier Builders
**Lever**: The creation of new technical or social capabilities.
**Function**: Accelerating the system clock speed. They create the "shocks" that test institutional adaptive capacity.
**Seed**: Sam Altman.

### 3. Ideological Architects
**Lever**: The construction of narratives, political alignments, and legal frameworks.
**Function**: Defining the "Sacred Narratives." They provide the map that determines if a system attempts a Return-to-Foundations or a radical Transformation.
**Seed**: Peter Thiel.

### 4. Infrastructure Operators
**Lever**: Control of the physical, computational, and logistical substrate.
**Function**: Managing the "Hard Layer." They maintain the throughput of the civilization; their failure is the literal definition of collapse (rapid simplification).
**Seed**: Satya Nadella.

## Methodology Notes
- **Literary-Saturated Inverse**: These subjects are heavily documented. Research must prioritize primary sources (letters, manifestos, interviews) over media commentary.
- **Narrative Sub-coding**: Principals are frequently coded as `rising-but-vulnerable` when their project's growth masks underlying structural fragility.
