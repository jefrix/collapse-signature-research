# Stream Three: Civilizational Foundation

**Purpose:** Historical case work, comparative audits, and literary-strategic analysis supporting the Collapse Signature framework.

This folder contains the project's core historical and civilizational research. It is distinct from methodology proposals (which live in `methodology/`) and from real-time financial/technocratic mapping (Streams One and Two).

## Contents

### Case Briefs
Individual or comparative historical case studies. These are the primary analytical units.

Examples:
- `late-ottoman/late-ottoman-comparative-brief-2026-05-13.md`
- `habsburg-collapse-brief.md`

### Audits & Comparative Work
Cross-case evidence collection and pattern analysis. These files synthesize findings across multiple cases rather than analyzing one in depth.

Current examples:
- `elite-self-indictment-audit.md` — Cross-case audit of elite self-indictment signals as a potential leading indicator.

### Literary Corpus Notes
Analysis of literary, philosophical, and cultural texts as strategic signals.

Examples:
- `late-tang-literary-corpus.md`

## Organization Principles

- Case-specific work should generally live in its own subfolder (e.g., `late-ottoman/`, `habsburg/`).
- Cross-case audits and comparative studies can live at the top level of this folder.
- Literary and textual analysis is grouped under literary corpus notes.
- Pure methodological proposals (variable definitions, coding schemas, etc.) belong in `methodology/`, not here.
