# Stream Two: Larry Fink (Finance-Class Principal)

Confidence Level: High (Milken Institute/CFR Synthesis)

Date: May 7, 2026

## Strategic Framework

Fink is repositioning BlackRock as the "physical layer" of the intelligence age, emphasizing hard infrastructure over abstract capital.

## Financializing the Buildout

### Compute Futures

At the May 2026 Milken Global Conference, Fink declared raw computing power a scarce resource to be priced via "Compute Futures".

### The $10 Trillion Gap

Estimates a $10 trillion requirement for AI infrastructure, noting that because governments are "too broke" to fund this, private markets must take total control of the buildout.

### The K-Shaped Collapse

Warns of a terminal gap where wage growth never matches AI capital returns.

### Stability Condition

He proposes "broadened ownership" as the only signature of stability; without it, the system faces rapid simplification (collapse).

## Analysis: The Intersection Flag

A critical friction point exists between Altman's Stabilizers and Fink's Financialization. Altman is building a safety net to catch the victims of the very infrastructure Fink is currently financializing into a "once-in-a-century" asset class.
