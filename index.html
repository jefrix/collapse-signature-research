The Collapse Signature — Cross-Stream Synthesis

The signature is the conjunction of three measurable conditions: high chokepoint concentration, active rising-narrative construction by named principals, and concentrated cross-channel exposure. Today's US AI infrastructure system shows all three. The Late Qing showed all three before 1898. The Late Tang showed two of three and survived simplified for 144 years.
