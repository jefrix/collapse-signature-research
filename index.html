An inquiry into civilizational and financial fragility

8 May 2026 · In Review

An ongoing multi-agent investigation into the structural patterns that precede systemic collapse — across civilizations, capital architectures, and the literary records left in their wake. The project pairs historical case studies (Late Bronze Age, Late Antiquity, Tang and Ming China, the Mongol fragmentation) with real-time mapping of the contemporary AI capital web. Methodology v3 is now canonical. Cycle II closes 15 May with the first formal synthesis.
